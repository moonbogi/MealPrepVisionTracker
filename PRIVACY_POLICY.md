# Privacy Policy for Meal Prep Vision Tracker

**Last Updated: November 28, 2025**

## Introduction

Meal Prep Vision Tracker ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Ingredient Data**: When you scan ingredients using the camera, the app processes images on your device to identify food items.
- **Recipe Preferences**: Recipe selections and favorites you choose within the app.
- **Meal Plans**: Meal planning data you create and save.

### Automatically Collected Information
**We do NOT collect or transmit any data from your device.** All processing happens locally on your device.

## How We Use Your Information

All data remains on your device and is used solely to:
- Identify ingredients through image recognition
- Suggest relevant recipes based on your pantry
- Track your nutrition information
- Provide meal planning functionality
- Improve your user experience

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage Only**: All your data is stored locally on your device using Core Data.
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store any of your information on external servers.
- **No User Accounts**: The app does not require account creation or login.

## Camera and Photo Library Access

### Camera Permission
- **Purpose**: To scan and identify ingredients in real-time
- **Usage**: Camera access is only active when you're using the scanning feature
- **Storage**: Images are processed immediately and optionally saved to your device
- **Control**: You can revoke camera access anytime in iOS Settings

### Photo Library Permission
- **Purpose**: To save scanned ingredient photos to your device (optional)
- **Usage**: Only when you choose to save an image
- **Control**: You can revoke photo library access anytime in iOS Settings

## Third-Party Services

**We do not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.** The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with external servers.

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with anyone.** Since all data remains on your device, there is nothing to share.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data remains on the device and we collect no personal information, the app is safe for users of all ages.

## Data Security

- All data is protected by iOS's built-in security features
- Data is encrypted at rest using iOS encryption
- No data transmission to external servers
- You control all data through iOS device management

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

### Access
All your data is accessible within the app at any time

### Deletion
You can delete data in two ways:
1. **Within the App**: Delete individual ingredients or meal plans
2. **Complete Deletion**: Uninstall the app to remove all associated data

### Export
Currently, data export is not supported, but data remains accessible within the app

## Data Retention

Data is retained on your device until you:
- Manually delete it within the app
- Uninstall the application
- Reset your device

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Notifying you through the app if changes are significant

## Machine Learning and AI

The app uses Apple's Vision framework for on-device image recognition:
- All image processing happens locally on your device
- No images or data are sent to external servers
- Apple's Vision framework respects your privacy
- Processing uses your device's Neural Engine when available

## Technical Details

### Permissions Required

**Camera** (NSCameraUsageDescription)
- **Required**: No (but core feature requires it)
- **Purpose**: Ingredient scanning and recognition
- **When Prompted**: First time accessing camera feature
- **Frequency**: Only when using scan feature

**Photo Library** (NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription)
- **Required**: No
- **Purpose**: Save scanned ingredient photos
- **When Prompted**: First time saving a photo
- **Frequency**: Only when saving images

### Data Types Stored Locally

1. **Ingredient Records**
   - Name, category, quantity
   - Confidence score from recognition
   - Optional photo (if saved)
   - Date added

2. **Meal Plans**
   - Date and meal type
   - Associated recipes
   - Servings and notes

3. **Preferences**
   - App settings
   - Onboarding completion status
   - UI preferences

### Data Security Measures

- iOS Keychain for sensitive preferences (if any)
- Core Data encryption at rest
- No network transmission
- Secure enclave utilization when available
- App sandbox isolation
